Uzbek traditional dish recipes

Uzbekistan is a country in Central Asia with a rich and diverse culture that has been influenced by a variety of historical and cultural factors. The traditional clothing of Uzbekistan is known for its bright colors and intricate embroidery. Men often wear long, loose-fitting tunics called "chapan," while women wear long dresses called "parandja," which are often adorned with gold and silver embroidery.

Traditional Uzbek foods include plov (a dish made with rice, vegetables, and meat), shurpa (a spicy soup made with meat and vegetables), and lagman (a noodle dish with meat and vegetables). Other popular dishes include samsa (meat-filled pastries), manti (dumplings filled with meat or vegetables), and kazy (a type of sausage). Bread is also an important part of the Uzbek diet, and a variety of flatbreads are commonly eaten
